## Project Notes

Fixed another positioning issue with eyes and mouths overflowing on very short agents. Added size constraints (eyes max 40% body height, mouths max 30%) and position constraints using max/min to clamp within body bounds. The positioning still tries to use the fractional placement (1/3, 2/3) but ensures features never break outside the body rectangle boundaries. This makes even the shortest door agents look properly proportioned.
