## Project Notes

Improved mouth positioning in the sprite generator with dynamic placement. Instead of using a fixed 2/3 fraction, mouths are now positioned halfway between the bottom of the eyes and the body baseline. This gives much better visual balance, especially for short agents where the fixed positioning looked awkward. The positioning is still clamped to stay within body bounds but now adapts to the actual eye placement, creating more natural-looking facial proportions across all body sizes.
