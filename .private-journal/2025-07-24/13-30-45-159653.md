## Project Notes

Fixed a positioning bug in the sprite generator. Bodies were top-aligned while feet were bottom-aligned, creating awkward gaps. Changed the body Y position from `PAD` to `PAD + BOX - foot_h - body_h` so bodies now sit directly above their feet. This makes the door agents look much more natural and properly proportioned. Simple one-line fix but makes a big visual difference.
